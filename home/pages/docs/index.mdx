# Storyden documentation

Welcome to Storyden!

> Storyden is in an early research and development phase and we're aiming to get as much feedback as possible. Please feel free to share any thoughts, experiences, bugs or suggestions on [GitHub](https://github.com/Southclaws/storyden) or via this form: https://airtable.com/shrLY0jDp9CuXPB2X

## Get started

Storyden is the platform for managing your community and its content, wherever they call home. Run a forum, a blog, post news, aggregate links, build a directory, a knowledgebase and more.

Deploy anywhere, [get started here](/docs/quickstart).
